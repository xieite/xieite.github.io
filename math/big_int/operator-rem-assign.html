xte::big_int<T>::operator%=()

Defined in header <xte/math/big_int.hpp>


Definitions

#0 
constexpr xte::big_int<T>& operator%=(const xte::big_int<T>& rhs) & noexcept(false);
Assigns the remainder after division by rhs. May throw if allocation fails or if rhs is zero.



Example

constexpr auto u64_max = 18446744073709551615ull;
auto n = xte::big_int("-340282366920938463426481119284349108228");
n %= u64_max;
std::println("{}", n);
Output: 
-3
